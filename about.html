About Utop Web

Utop Web was founded in 2021 with the purpose of making tech more accessible through affordable and engaging online tech education. Over the course of the following decade, we’ve taught thousands of people how to code and design. Today, Treehouse is the educational platform of choice for thousands of students, schools, libraries, non-profits, and corporations around the globe. Practical, Interactive Session

Interesting as it may be, you’re not going to find any course material covering programming self-driving cars, creating your own programming language, or building a Rubik’s Cube-solving robot. Instead, we focus exclusively on topics, languages, and technologies that are used within high-salary technology roles. Programmers with knowledge of JavaScript, Angular, HTML, CSS, React, Vue and more are in extraordinarily high demand, and so that’s what we teach. We also focus on other critical parts of the web development stack, including design, user experience, databases, and product management.
